Pancakes

Description

Fluffy and delicious pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together wet ingredients.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease with butter.
  5. Pour batter onto the skillet to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
