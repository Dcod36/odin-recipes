Pizza

Description

A classic Italian pizza with tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C)
  2. Roll out pizza dough
  3. Add sauce and toppings
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until golden brown
